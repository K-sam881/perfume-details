Fragrance Name

Radiant Bloom - A luxurious floral scent that captures the essence of sophistication and elegance.

Ingredients

- Top Notes: Bergamot, Mandarin Orange

- Heart Notes: Rose, Jasmine, Lily of the Valley

- Base Notes: Vanilla, Musk, Amber

Usage Instructions

Apply to pulse points such as wrists, neck, and behind the ears for a long-lasting fragrance. Avoid spraying directly on clothes to prevent staining.

Safety Information

- For external use only.

- Keep out of reach of children.

- Avoid contact with eyes. In case of contact, rinse immediately with water.
