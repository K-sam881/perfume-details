Luxury Perfume Details

Fragrance Name: Mystique Essence

Ingredients: A harmonious blend of jasmine, sandalwood, bergamot, and vanilla for a sophisticated and long-lasting aroma.

Usage Instructions:

  • Spray lightly on pulse points such as wrists, neck, and behind the ears.
  • Avoid rubbing the perfume into the skin to preserve the scent layers.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Safety Information:

  • For external use only.
  • Keep away from children.
  • Avoid contact with eyes and irritated skin.
  • Perform a patch test before first use.

About the Fragrance:

Mystique Essence is a tribute to elegance and mystery. Crafted with the finest ingredients, this perfume embodies the essence of luxury and timeless beauty.
